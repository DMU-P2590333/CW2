About Me

I am a student at De Montfort University in Leicester, I am currently studying Media Production. I grew up in a little village in the middle of nowhere, surrounded by fields. This sparked my love for nature and landscape photography at a very young age. My love for film and television started at a young age too, this was something that grew into a true passion of mine, it wasn't really until secondary school that I realised that I could work towards getting a career in the field. Throughout my educational career I have met some amazing teachers who helped me on my way, even believing in me when I couldn't believe in myself. I have taken many interests in the different paths available in the industry, I have written my own scripts, produced my own works, edited, directed and many other things. Being pushed to be a one man team to complete a production during my time at college definitely gave me some experience in each field.
