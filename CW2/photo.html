Missing Minds

"Missing Minds" was a production piece I had to create during my time at sixthform, The piece had to be a minute long teaser trailer to one of our larger projects. It focused on the effects on cinematography than anything else. Not only was this my first production, but I also produced it without any team, no camera or light operators, no editor. Everything was done by me, with a strict time limit. I am proud of this piece because of that, regardless of the quality.

Dead Mans Hate

One of my first university assignments was to create a 2 minute audio piece telling a story of our choice, I decided to do a reading of "Dead Mans Hate" a poem written by Robert Ervin Howard. I added the sound effects, which were a mix of sourced pieces and foley sounds I recorded myself. I then edited the piece to my standard in Adobe Audition
